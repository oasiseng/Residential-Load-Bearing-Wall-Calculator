Disclaimer: This tool provides estimates per IRC 2021 prescriptive provisions and NDS 2018 ASD for axial compression in dimension lumber studs. It does not check combined bending + axial interaction (NDS §3.9), lateral/shear forces, eccentric loading, or seismic provisions. The exterior “wind adjustment” is a simplified 20% reduction — a full wind analysis per ASCE 7 is required for exterior wall design.
